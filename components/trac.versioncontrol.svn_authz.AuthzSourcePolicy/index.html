trac.versioncontrol.svn_authz.AuthzSourcePolicy

Permission policy for `source:` and `changeset:` resources using a Subversion authz file. `FILE_VIEW` and `BROWSER_VIEW` permissions are granted as specified in the authz file. `CHANGESET_VIEW` permission is granted for changesets where `FILE_VIEW` is granted on at least one modified file, as well as for empty changesets.
